My Promise

Falcons, Griffins, friends, lend me your ears, 4 score and 8 years ago I had a dream that America's best high school united under one goal to take as many ap tests as humanly possible would vote me for asb president. Now this was no ordinary dream, for those of you that recall the prophetic moles that visited me last year I recently had a repeat visit. And this clan of prophetic moles told me that to truly change Saratoga for the better I would have to become ASB president. Every night, as I lie in bed trying to figure out whether the dress was blue or gold, I also think about what it would mean to be ASB president. Being ASB president would put me at the forefront of the student body to make decisions on behalf of the student body that would benefit all of us. And when voted ASB president that is exactly what I intend to do. When looking at your ballots consider why are these people really running for office. I cannot speak for anyone else but for myself its definitely not about college apps, because lets be honest, with my grades im not getting into college anyways. I am running because I care about this school and what happens to it. You want lights on the basketball and tennis courts then vote me for asb president. You want more spirit throughout school then vote me for ASB president. You want better Cafeteria food then talk to the lunch ladies.

So vote me for asb president and you really will have something to #thanksasb about.
